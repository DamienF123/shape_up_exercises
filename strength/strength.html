Strength Training

Strength training is a type of physical exercise that uses resistance to induce contraction. This builds strength, anaerobic endurance, and size of skeletal muscles

Benefits

When properly performed, strength training can provide significant functional benefits and improvement in overall health and well-being. Some of the benefits are:

  • increased bone, muscle, tendon, and ligament strength and toughness
  • improved joint function
  • reduced potential for injury
  • increased bone density
  • increased metabolism
  • improved cardiac function

Exercises and sports

Training commonly uses technique of progressively increasing the force output of the muscle through incremental weight increases. It uses a variety of exercises and types of equiptment to target specific muscle groups.

Some sports where strength training is central are bodybuilding, weightlifing, shotput, discus throw, and javelin throw. Many other sports use strength training regimen. Thses include tennis, American football, wrestling, track and field, rowing, lacrosse, basketball, hockey, and soccer
