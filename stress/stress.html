Stress Relief

What is stress?

Stress is the feeling that people have when they are overloaded and struggling to cope with demands. Anything that poses a real or percived challenge or threat to a persons's well-being can cause stress.

Stress can also be a motivator. It can be essential to survival. The "fight-or-flight" mechanism help us know when and how to respond to danger. But when this mechanism is triggered too easily, or when there are too many stressors at one time, it can become harmful to a parson's mental and physical health.

How to manage your stress

To help manage stress, try the following:

  • Take a break from what your're doing
  • Exercise on a regular basis
  • Take time to smile and laugh
  • Get support from family and friends
  • Meditate
